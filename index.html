How My Scripts Increase Retention

Every script I write is engineered to keep viewers watching. Here are the core retention principles I use.

Pattern Interrupt Hooks

The first 5–10 seconds challenge a belief or create curiosity so viewers stop scrolling and pay attention.

Open Loops

Important ideas are introduced early but resolved later, creating curiosity that keeps viewers watching.

Identity Triggers

Scripts frame problems around identity so viewers feel personally involved in the story.

Psychology-Based Storytelling

Concepts like dopamine, habits, and decision-making are used to make ideas feel logical and memorable.
